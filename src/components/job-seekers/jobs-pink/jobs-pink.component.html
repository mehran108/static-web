What is Jobs Pink?

Jobs Pink is a job board available for free for job seekers to find the job that fits their needs, all at the comfort of their computers. Job seekers can upload their resumes, learn about the job, and share the job all from one, easy-to-use website.

What makes Jobs Pink different?

Jobs Pink is a job board made for the GTA and surrounding area to help target job seekers employers need. Unlike larger job boards, Jobs Pink is affordable and helps reach out to thousands of qualified candidates everyday. Employers can now save advertising costs as they are instantly connected to the candidates they are looking for.
